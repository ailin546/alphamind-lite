# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## 1. What type of teams is this tool designed for?

Built for startup teams and innovation departments obsessed with exceptional product experience. Whether you're building hardware, software, or services—if you reject mediocrery and crave to "think different," this is your creative crucible.

## 2. Do I need a programming background to use it?

Not at all. We believe great products come from intuition and aesthetics, not code. Our interface follows the "one-click" principle, letting creativity flow without technical barriers.

## 3. How do I start my first project?

Click the "+" button on the main screen, choose a project template or start from a blank canvas. Beginners should start with the "48-Hour Prototype" template—it helps you validate ideas fast.

## 4. Does it support real-time collaboration?

Yes. Up to 10 people can collaborate online simultaneously, each with a unique cursor color. We've specially designed "Soul Mode"—showing only core elements to avoid visual noise distracting from creativity.

## 6. What's the difference between free and paid plans?

The free tier lets you create 3 projects with basic collaboration features. The paid version unlocks unlimited projects, advanced AI assistance, version history, and "Reality Distortion Field"—our premium creative enhancement mode.

## 7. How do I export my work?

Export in multiple formats: PDF presentations, interactive prototype links, Figma/Sketch source files. Your work should shine anywhere.

## 8. Is there a mobile app?

Currently available in iOS beta, with Android in development. Mobile focuses on "Idea Capture"—jot down thoughts anytime, then refine them on desktop.

## 9. How can I get product feedback?

Join our "Innovators Community"—featuring seasoned product folks from Apple, Pixar, and Tesla. Every Friday there's an online critique session where your work faces real scrutiny.
